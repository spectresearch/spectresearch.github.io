About us

SPECT Research is an organization focused in research and provision of services in Chile. If you need help in security issues affecting your organization or you need a new software to automate tasks, please write us an email.
Examples of services SPECT Research can give are:

  • Research to solve complex problems
  • Penetration testing
  • Ethical hacking of desktop/web applications
  • Implementation of security tools
  • Web/Desktop software development
  • Technical training (security & software development)
