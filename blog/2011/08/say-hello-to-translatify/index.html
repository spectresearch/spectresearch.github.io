Say Hello to Translatify

06 August 2011 on development

Two weeks ago I started the development of Translatify, a Google Chrome extension to make easy the translation of words inside the web pages. Today, I release the first version at Google Chrome Web Store. It's a open-source project, you can check how to contribute and report bugs in its page at SPECT Labs.

Thanks to Rodrigo González for the support and Ignacio Mella for report bugs and ask for necessary features.

Enjoy it!

Claudio Salazar
