Our first university course

19 March 2013 on security, education

Today is a great day, I (Claudio Salazar) can officially confirm that this semester I'm going to teach a security course at Universidad Técnica Federico Santa María - Valparaíso Campus, where I got my degree in Computer Science.

The undergraduate course is named "Taller de Seguridad Web" (Web Security Workshop) and it considers twelve classes, two of them practical. It's a new course which covers latest trends in attack and defense of web applications.

All the resources related to this course can be reached through education section of SPECT Research blog. The first lesson is available to check the goals and scheduling of the course.

Claudio Salazar
