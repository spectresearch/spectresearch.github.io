Our story

23 June 2010

SPECT Research was born at early 2006, due to the interest of two students in the research in electronics and computer science. That interest gave origin to this blog, which was immutable for a long time but it has now returned.

One of SPECT Research's founders took up again the project
in the middle of 2009, with the goal of identifying his works beneath the concept of an identity for the acknowledgement among his clients and researchers. SPECT Research wants to grow up on the time, adding new researchers to the team.

Along with our research line, we offer advanced services in computer science to satisfy specific needs of companies nowadays.

SPECT Research has several projects in development and through this blog we'll show our progress and interesting issues in the process.

Stay tuned.

Claudio Salazar
